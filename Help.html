General FAQ:

What programming languages do you offer tutoring or consulting for?

Fundamentals and Intermediate Courses on C, C++, C#, Python, HTML, CSS, and Java. We also do consults on the said languages.

Can I choose a specific tutor/consultant to work with?

Yes, it depends on your preference. We have a list of tutors/consultants that you can choose from.

How long are the tutoring or consulting sessions, and what is the format?

The duration of the session is indicated in our fixed courses while our consults and specific tutoring depend on customer agreement.

What resources or materials will I need for the sessions?

A laptop, a decent internet connection, and your eagerness to learn. Course Materials such as PPT, handouts, etc. are provided by the consultants.

Clients are responsible for providing accurate and timely information required for AlVilla Consulting to deliver
